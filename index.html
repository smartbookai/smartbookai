VISION

At MCER Lab, we leverage advanced AI, robotics, and automation to solve educational challenges. Our mission is to develop intelligent, adaptable solutions focused on personalized learning, student behavior analysis, and classroom interaction enhancement.

We specialize in data-driven models using ML, DL, and RL, with a focus on robotics for real-time decision-making in educational settings. Our goal is to explore AI's potential in improving engagement, material delivery, and classroom management.

Committed to human-centric AI, we design solutions that complement educators and students, enhancing outcomes while promoting ethical AI practices. Through interdisciplinary collaborations, we aim to deliver actionable insights for better educational practices globally.

Equity & Inclusion
